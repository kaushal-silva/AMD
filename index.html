Client Onboarding: First Steps

Help us understand your health by completing one of the steps below.

1. Impaired Mouth Syndrome Questionnaire

Answer 5 quick questions about your daily symptoms and habits.

Q1: Do you frequently wake up with a dry mouth or headache?

Q2: Do you often experience neck pain, shoulder tension, or poor posture?

Q3: Do you have difficulty chewing or experience jaw joint (TMJ) discomfort?

Q4: Do you notice teeth grinding or clenching during the day or night?

Q5: Do you frequently use mouthwash or chewing gum to manage dry mouth/bad breath?

2. Upload Photos / Scans

Upload recent photos of your teeth, jaw, or posture, or past scans.
